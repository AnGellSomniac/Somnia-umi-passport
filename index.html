⚡ XP, Battle Power & Glory
XP (Experience)
GM = 100 XP base
3-day streak = 110 XP
5-day streak = 125 XP
7+ day streak = 150 XP
Loss = +2 XP

Level = XP ÷ 100 + 1
Battle Power (BP)
BP = Weight × (Level + 10) × (Streak + 1)

Determines your rank within winners.
Higher BP = higher tier = bigger reward.
Glory Points
Glory = XP×0.4 + PTS×0.6

Monthly total determines NFT winner.
Top-1 Glory = mint exclusive NFT.
🛡 Key Rules
🔒 Anti-Snipe: Voting locks 5 minutes before round ends
⏰ 24h Claim: Rewards expire 24 hours after resolve
🌫 Fog of War: Side counts hidden during battle — only total warriors shown
⚖ Tie Breaker: If weights are equal, PROTECT wins (defender advantage)
🔥 Streaks: Missing a day resets your battle streak to 0
💀 Expired Claims: Unclaimed tokens roll over to the next day's pool

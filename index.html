The Chronicles of Somnia

A saga in seven chapters · Add your own illustrations

CHAPTER I
The Fracture

In the beginning, there was only Somnia — a realm where dreams took form and every sleeping mind wove threads of the tapestry. All souls belonged to one house. There was no theft, no defense, only creation.

Then came the Fracture. A shard of waking consciousness pierced the dream, and from its edges bloomed two desires: to take, and to keep.

CHAPTER II
The House of Attack

From the fracture rose the Bold — warriors who believed the world was meant to be seized, not inherited. They forged blades from lightning and wore crimson for the blood of conquest.

"What is given can be taken," their first champion declared. "What is taken, is earned."

CHAPTER III
The House of Protect

In the shadow of the Bold, another house awakened. The Guardian. They wove shields from moonlight and wore violet for the depth of patience. They did not create. They did not take. They held.

"The tide recedes, but the shore remains," whispered their eldest. "We are the shore."

CHAPTER IV
The Eternal Hourglass

The first war between the houses lasted a thousand years. When neither side could triumph, the ancients forged the Hourglass — a sacred vessel that rules each day.

When the sands empty, the victors are chosen. When the sands refill, the battle begins anew. This is the Law of the Arena.

CHAPTER V
The Passport of Souls

Every warrior who enters the Chronicles must first forge their Passport — a binding of identity to the dream-realm. No passport, no battle. No battle, no glory.

Your name becomes eternal. Your victories, chronicled. Your defeats, remembered.

CHAPTER VI
The Morning Ritual

Before any warrior may step into the Arena, they must perform the Morning Ritual — a sacred offering to the Treasury that binds them to this day's battle.

Those who ritual seven days without fail earn the ancient streak blessing — more experience, more glory, greater destiny.

CHAPTER VII
Your Chapter Begins

The old chapters are written in stone. The next one is written in dreams. In blood. In glory.

The hourglass empties at midnight. The arena awaits its next champion.

Will it be you?
